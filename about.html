About St. Thomas Junior School

Nurturing Excellence, Building Character, Inspiring Success

Our Story

Founded in 1995, St. Thomas Junior School has been a beacon of educational excellence in our community for over 25 years. What started as a small educational initiative has grown into one of the most respected primary schools in the region.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide quality education that nurtures not just academic excellence but also character development and moral values that will guide our students throughout their lives.

Today, we continue to uphold these founding principles while embracing modern educational methodologies and technologies to prepare our students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, quality education that develops the intellectual, physical, social, and moral potential of every child in a nurturing Christian environment that promotes excellence and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution that produces confident, creative, and responsible citizens who contribute positively to society while maintaining strong moral values and academic excellence.

Principal's Message

Mrs. Millicent Otieno

School Principal

Welcome to St. Thomas Junior School, where we believe every child is unique and deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can grow academically, socially, and morally.

We pride ourselves on our holistic approach to education, combining academic rigor with character development, creativity, and critical thinking skills. Our small class sizes ensure personalized attention, while our modern facilities and innovative teaching methods prepare our students for success in an ever-changing world.

I invite you to join our school family and experience the difference that quality education, caring teachers, and a supportive community can make in your child's life.

Our Journey

1995 - Foundation

St. Thomas Junior School was established with just 50 students and 5 teachers.

2005 - Expansion

Major infrastructure development and introduction of computer lab and library.

2015 - CBC Implementation

Successfully transitioned to the Competency-Based Curriculum system.

2020 - Digital Learning

Introduced modern digital learning platforms and smart classrooms.
